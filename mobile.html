Help & Support

How to Request a Book

  1. Browse our available books in the catalog
  2. Use search to find books you're interested in
  3. Tap the "+" button to open the request form
  4. Fill in your details and submit the request
  5. We'll notify you when your book is available

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a book?
Typically, books are available within 2-3 working days.

Can I request multiple books?
Yes, you can request up to 3 books at a time.

How long can I keep a book?
Books can be borrowed for 14 days with one renewal option.
